Alpaca Game

Rules

Each player picks 3 spells from the list to train their app to recognize. They then complete five rounds where they pick a spell to do. Whoever's spell wins (described below) gets one point.
  • A spell intended to affect the body wins over one not intended to affect the body, except for expelliarmus and protego, which stop the offensive spell from happening.
  • If an offensive spell is blocked by protego or expelliarmus, 1 point is given to the defender.
  • If an attacking spell successfully lands, 3 points are given to the attacker.
  • If there is no attack, nothing happens, no one gets any points.
Spells:
    Attack Spells
  • Mimblewimble
  • Locomotor
  • Stupefy
  • Incendio
  • Oppugno
    Defensive Spells
  • Wingardium Leviosa
  • Arresto Momentum
  • Finite Incantatem
  • Expelliarmus
  • Aquamenti
